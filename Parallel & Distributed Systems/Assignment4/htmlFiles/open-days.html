WHAT YOU CAN EXPECT FROM OPEN DAY 2020

 

This is not our usual Open Day… Usually you would able to visit us in person so we could have a chat about your courses on campus and show off our facilities.

This year we’re coming to you.

We’ll be using our website to deliver our  Open Day presentations and YouTube to show you around our campus and facilities.

The presentations will be in video or PDF format and we will have experts available to answer your questions through our online chats and Q&A’s.

You will be able to choose how you engage with us, so once you are registered (above) you can join us RIGHT HERE from 10:30am, 25 April 2020 for information about studying at MU and chats with staff members and students.

All from the couch, in your jammies.

The Maynooth team will do our best to answer all your questions and we hope to meet you all in person soon.

Talks & Presentations

Our traditional presentations will be available in video and downloadable via PDF for those with poor connectivity. Talks will be available from here and will cover, among other things:

 

  • Courses & subject areas
  • Fees & services
  • Accommodation
  • Student Services
  • Budgeting
  • Library
  • Student ambassadors
  • Campus facilities

Questions

Once you’ve had a look through the presentations that interest you, you will be able to to ask questions in our chats and Q&A’s

You can also talk to existing Maynooth students who could give you an idea of what it’s REALLY like to join Maynooth
